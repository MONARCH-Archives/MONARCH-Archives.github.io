Clearance Levels

Personnel clearance ranges from Level 1 (Bronze) to BLACK-5. Only top officials are granted full BLACK-5 access, allowing entry to the deepest sections of the Field Manual.

Clearance Level Code Name Access Description
Level 0 CIVILIAN No access. General public.
Level 1 BRONZE Basic field knowledge. Entry-level personnel.
Level 2 SILVER Standard operative clearance. Access to field data and monster classifications.
Level 3 GOLD Division officers and tech engineers. Access to mission reports and containment data.
Level 4 PLATINUM High-level command. Access to black-ops deployments and recovered kaiju biology.
Level 5 ONYX Tactical directors. Access to weapon systems, experimental tech, and A.C.O.S. logs.
Level 6 BLACK-1 Restricted mission briefings. Partial redacted files and Alpha-class encounters.
Level 7 BLACK-2 Full access to black-site labs and minor classified events.
Level 8 BLACK-3 Access to classified bestiary entries and failed containment protocols.
Level 9 BLACK-4 Access to legacy threat data and long-sealed M.O.N.A.R.C.H. operations.
Level 10 BLACK-5 Eyes Only. Top-tier clearance. Access to Titan-class awakening events, Mindburrower research, and The Chasm of Nhagul. Only 5 individuals confirmed to have full BLACK-5 clearance.
