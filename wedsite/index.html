Organizational Overview

M.O.N.A.R.C.H. – Monster Observation, Neutralization, and Raid Containment Headquarters – is a global paramilitary force tasked with defending Earth from titanic threats. Formed in the wake of the First Emergence, its members operate in total secrecy under the highest black-level clearance.

Kaiju Files

  • The Sleeper Beneath – Buried ancient leviathan in stasis.
  • The Cloudpiercer – Skyborne kaiju larger than most aircraft carriers.
  • The Hollow Maw – Oceanic predator with bioluminescent lure mechanisms.
  • The Mindburrowers – Parasitic intelligence controlling hosts like the Mountain Walker.
  • The Dust Wyrm (Sandcoil) – Juvenile subterranean predator.
  • Titanus Rhagorran – Titan-class apex sand drake, currently dormant.
  • The Mountain Walker – Peaceful megafauna used as camouflage by parasites.

Gear & Vehicles

Operatives wear heavy armor with crimson-highlighted plating and division-marked helmets. All gear includes comms, utility belts, and sidearms. Vehicles include:

  • Humvees: Armored, transport-capable with mounted turrets and logos on doors.
  • Tanks: Twin-tread heavy armor with mounted MGs and turret insignia.
  • Helicopters: Sleek, multi-role stealth choppers with gunmetal-grey plating.

Divisions

M.O.N.A.R.C.H. is split into specialized divisions including:

  • R.A.Z.O.R. – Rapid Aerial Zoning & Orbital Response
  • G.L.A.S.S. – Geological Logistics & Subterranean Survey
  • B.L.A.D.E. – Battlefield Logistics, Assault & Deployment Enforcement

Redacted Logs

Classified mission failures, black ops intel, and death logs are held in Section 07 of the manual. Examples include:

  • Operation Sandspire – Sandcoil ambush report
  • Nhagul Depth Recon – No survivors
  • Project: Mindlink Collapse – Redacted beyond BLACK-4

Clearance Levels

Personnel clearance ranges from Level 1 (Bronze) to BLACK-5. Only top officials are granted full BLACK-5 access, allowing entry to the deepest sections of the Field Manual.
