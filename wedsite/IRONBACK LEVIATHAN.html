“You don’t kill it. You just hope it’s not hungry.”

  • Class: GIANT
  • Weight: Estimated to be 190,000 metric tons, supported by its dense, armour-plated exoskelleton and massive bone stucture
  • Origin:Belived to have originated from a subglacial trench ecosystem
  • Length: Estimated 180m (aquatic)
  • Behavioral Tags: Guardian, Hive-Linked
  • Status: Active – Patrols the Mariana Rift
  • Last seen:Last sighted submerged off the coast of Kamchatka
  • First Contact: Detected via sonar anomalies in deep Pacific trench. Appears to regulate oceanic thermal vents and drives off rival species. Rumored to have destroyed a Chinese nuclear sub—no official records confirm.
  • Tactical Notes: Armor plates across dorsal shell deflect most torpedoes and anti-kaiju ordinance. Harpoons bounce. Teeth marks recovered on giant squid corpses suggest pressure strength exceeding industrial compaction machinery.
  • Containment Status: None. Avoidance strategy enacted. Oceanic patrol drones programmed to turn back at 1,000 meters from projected territory.
